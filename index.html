Introduction

My name is Md Kawsher Mahbub, and I am now pursuing a PhD in computer science at Kent State University. I worked as a lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at BUBT. My goal is to work in an innovative way to improve education. I appreciate learning about new technological breakthroughs.

Biography

I began my academic journey with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Bangladesh University of Business and Technology (BUBT). My passion for research led me to pursue a PhD, where I am exploring advanced topics in AI and ML. Outside of academia, I enjoy, watching movies, tv series, traveling, cooking, and mentoring students in STEM fields.
