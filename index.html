Md Kawsher Mahbub

AI · ML · DL · Medical Imaging · XAI · Health Informatics

Md Kawsher Mahbub is an educator and researcher with a Master’s in Computer Science from Kent State University, USA, and prior experience as a Lecturer at BUBT. He has more than three years of university-level teaching and mentoring experience spanning lectures, labs, and project supervision. His research interests include artificial intelligence, health informatics, deep learning, and computer vision, with 11 peer-reviewed publications across reputable journals and conferences. He also serves as a peer reviewer for IEEE CBMS and Tech Science Press journals and has held leadership roles, including Secretary of the Bangladesh Student Association at Kent State University. His objective is to pursue an academic career as a Lecturer/Assistant Professor in Computer Science & Engineering, leveraging his teaching experience, research expertise, and passion for innovation to mentor students, produce impactful research, and advance institutional excellence
